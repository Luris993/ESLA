about us

Esport Legal Advice (ESLA) is the legal organisation strictly dedicated to esports. The knowledge and quality services we provide, help build trust and confidence in the esports environment. We combines many years of experience of legal counsellors and lawyers who represent the values appropriate for ESLA. We offer legal advice and support for all esports projects managed by our clients.

Throughout our core values – integrity, communication, simplicity and logic – and our understanding of esports sector, we create best legal solutions to help our clients with building their secure esports future. Right through the legal service, we take time to understand clearly what matters to our clients and we build strong relationships with them, based on trust and respect.

ESLA is created to support our clients in achieving their goals. For this reason, we are permanently working to improve the quality of our services.
